## Decision
The composition is: normalizeText FIRST, then chunkText on the normalized string, then map each chunk through wordCount. The new module goes at apps/web/src/lib/prepare-for-synthesis.ts alongside its test file apps/web/src/lib/prepare-for-synthesis.test.ts. No barrel/index update is needed per the issue scope.
